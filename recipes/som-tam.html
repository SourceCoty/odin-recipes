Melt-Your-Face-Off Spicy Som Tam

The Spiciest, Face-Meltiest, "Kor Ped Ped Na Krab" Thai Papaya Salad

A delicious Thai salad, Som Tam is a green papaya salad recipe that combines all four tastes - sour, chilli, sweet and salty. A healthy and delicious treat for brunch, lunch or a casual light dinner.

Ingredients

  • One small, green, raw papaya shredded
  • 4 long beans chopped
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • cherry tomatoes cut into quarters
  • 6 red or green chilies
  • 2 tablespoons palm sugar or 1 tablespoon regular sugar
  • 3 tablespoons lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce or soy sauce
  • 3 tablespoons ground peanuts roasted
  • fresh lettuce, green beans, lime wedges, and chillies garnish

Instructions

  1. Peel and seed papaya and shred it. Put in a bowl.
  2. Crush garlic with a pestle, then add long beans and tomatoes and pound a few times to release juices.
  3. Add the chillies and crush lightly to release the heat.
  4. In a separate cup dissolve the sugar in the lime juice and fish sauce.
  5. Mix all ingredients together now and toss to mix.
  6. Serve in a shallow bowl and top with garnish.
